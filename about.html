Welcome to the Microbe Quiz

This website is designed to help people study for the Microbe Mission event in Science Olympiad. Currently, there are various categories for MCQs that aim to test your knowledge of Microbiology, ranging from genetics to pathogenesis.

Each category contains questions that are either taken from past invitationals or made by me, Justin. The quiz provides immediate feedback on your answers to enhance learning.

Whether you're a student preparing for an exam or just exploring microbiology, this quiz is a fun and interactive way to strengthen your knowledge.

Click "Home" to start the quiz!
